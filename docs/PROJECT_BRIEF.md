## üåå Visual Direction

### Atmosphere
- **Dark & Mysterious:** Deep black/charcoal backgrounds
- **Neon Accents:** Electric blues, purples, cyans, and magentas
- **Futuristic:** Sci-fi inspired, digital, otherworldly
- **Premium Feel:** Sleek, polished, high-end aesthetic

### Animation Philosophy
- **Mesmerizing but not distracting:** Background motion should enhance, not overwhelm
- **Micro-interactions:** Every hover, click, and transition should feel alive
- **Smooth & fluid:** 60fps animations, eased transitions
- **Purpose-driven:** Animations guide attention and create delight

### Design Principles
- **Minimal text:** Let visuals speak
- **Maximum impact:** First impression matters
- **Mobile-first:** QR context means mobile priority
- **Fast loading:** Performance is non-negotiable

4. **Contact conversion:** Easy path to WhatsApp or call
5. **Memorability:** Visitor remembers the experience
